Fairway Focus Support

Welcome to the support page for Fairway Focus, your ultimate golf companion app for tracking shots, analyzing performance, and improving consistency. If you have questions or issues, we're here to help.

For any inquiries, bug reports, or feedback, please email us at: support@fairwayfocusapp.com. We aim to respond within 48 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I set up my golf bag?

    Go to the "Bag Setup" tab in the app. Tap "Manage Bag" to select clubs from categories like Drivers, Woods, Hybrids, Irons, and Wedges. Your selections are saved automatically.

  • How do I start a new test session?

    In the "Test" tab, enter the number of shots, minimum yardage, and maximum yardage. Tap "Start Test" to generate random targets. Submit each shot with club, distance, and deviation details.

  • What do the test scores mean?

    Scores range from 0-100, based on distance accuracy and deviation penalties. Green (80+) indicates excellent performance, yellow (50-80) is good with room for improvement, and red (below 50) suggests focusing on accuracy.

  • Why isn't my data saving or showing in History/Club Data?

    Data is stored locally on your device using UserDefaults. Ensure the app has storage permissions in your iPhone Settings. If issues persist, try force-quitting the app or restarting your device. Contact us with details if needed.

  • How can I reset my test history or bag setup?

    Currently, there's no in-app reset button. You can clear app data by uninstalling and reinstalling the app, but this will erase all saved progress. We're considering adding a reset feature in future updates.

  • What does the trend in History mean?

    The trend (Improving, Stable, Declining) is calculated from your recent 5 sessions using simple linear regression on scores. Use the insights and tips provided to improve.

  • How is club data calculated?

    Club stats like average distance, min/max, deviation, and score are aggregated from all your test shots. The score factors in dispersion and variance—higher is better (green for 80+, yellow 50-80, red below).

  • Does the app collect any personal data?

    No, all data is stored locally on your device and not shared with us or third parties. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.

  • The app crashed—what should I do?

    Update to the latest version via the App Store. If it persists, note what you were doing (e.g., submitting a shot) and email us with your device model and iOS version for assistance.

  • Can I suggest new features?

    Absolutely! We're always improving. Email your ideas to us—we value user feedback for future updates like voice input or on-course tracking.
